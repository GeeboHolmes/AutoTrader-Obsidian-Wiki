## Summary
A three-stage rule-based strategy (December 2023): H4 → 15m → 1m. The video explicitly walks through a live GBP/USD trade demonstrating all three stages, including re-entry on continuation when the first entry is missed. Key teaching point: probability of a reaction decreases the lower price gets into a price leg — the first CHoCH entry is highest probability; entries further down into the leg are lower probability because an inducement or reversal becomes increasingly likely.

## Key Points
- **Three stages:** H4 (directional bias) → 15m (structure confirmation) → 1m (entry model).
- **H4 bias:** Either playing a pullback after a structural swing high/low run, or playing a continuation. Use Fibonacci 50-78.6% on the H4 leg to predict pullback depth.
- **15m:** Map structure, identify CHoCH, determine OB/FVG area for entry.
- **1m entry model:** Enter from supply/demand zone after 1m CHoCH in trade direction. Minimum 1:5 RR required.
- **Probability decreasing rule:** "The lower that price comes down into a price leg, the lower the probability that price respects your areas." First CHoCH entry = highest probability. Entries further down = decreasing probability due to increasing risk of inducement.
- **Scaling / continuation entries:** After the first TP (50% at 1:5), scale in on subsequent CHoCH signals as price continues in direction.
- **Partials:** Take 50% at 1:5, let remaining 50% run to second TP (1:11–17 typical).

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **H4 bias:** Identify whether price has just taken a swing high/low (expect pullback) or is in continuation phase.
2. **H4 Fibonacci:** Draw Fibonacci from the H4 leg low to high (or high to low). Entry zone = 50–78.6%.
3. **15m:** Confirm CHoCH on 15m (close through swing point). Mark the OB/FVG in the CHoCH zone.
4. **1m:** Drop to 1m. Wait for a bullish (or bearish) structure shift inside the 15m OB zone.
5. **Entry:** Enter from the OB/FVG on 1m. Stop: beyond OB. TP1: 1:5. TP2: 1:11+.
6. **Break even:** Move stop to BE the moment the immediate structural low (for longs) is taken out after entry.
7. **Re-entry on miss:** If price misses the OB, remove order. Drop to 5m, find the next OB/FVG at the next structural level. Enter from there with a tighter stop.
8. **Skip late entries:** If already far into the price leg, do not enter. Probability of inducement is too high. Wait for price to complete the leg and give a new structural setup.
