## Key Points
- 3 steps: session bias (15m structure) → liquidity raid (Asia sweep) → LTF confirmation (1m CHoCH)
- Bonus filter: price must be in premium zone (above 50% fib of current price leg) for bearish setups
- Ideal bearish setup: Asia high swept + price in 61.8–78.6% Fibonacci premium zone
- Entry: 5m FVG (or OB)
- Stop: above the session high / OB high
- Target: equal lows / Asia low = 6.5 RR shown

- Step 3 is the 1m CHoCH: wait for 1m to shift from bullish to bearish (sellers take control)

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Session Bias:** 15m time frame. Identify most recent swing structure. Bearish = LH/LL. Look for pullback retracement into premium. If above 50% of current price leg = premium zone = bias for shorts.
2. **Step 2 — Liquidity Raid:** London session opens. Wait for Asia high (bearish) or Asia low (bullish) to be taken. Ideal bearish setup: Asia high swept while price is in the 61.8–78.6% Fibonacci premium zone. Extra confirmation: equal highs at Asia high level.
3. **Step 3 — LTF Confirmation:** Drop to 1m. Wait for 1m to shift from bullish to bearish (1m swing low taken = CHoCH to bearish). This is the green light. Go to 5m: find FVG (or OB) in the reversal zone. Enter short from FVG. Stop above session high. Target: equal lows / Asia low.
