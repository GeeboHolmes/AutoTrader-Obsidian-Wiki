## Summary
Entirely motivational/sales content. No new trading mechanics or strategy rules. References a San Diego School of Management study tracking 700,000 online gamblers (2019–2023), finding only 4% withdrew more than they deposited. Uses this as a parallel to trading: without a statistical edge, trading is equivalent to gambling. The "edge" is defined as a repeatable, backtested system with at least 800 trades over 5+ years of data.

## Key Points
- LK claims 5 years of trading data, 800+ trades, all following the same system.
- "Edge" = a statistical likelihood of success proven over hundreds of trades, not just recent performance.
- "Real trading is the most boring thing ever" — confirmation that edge-based trading is systematic and repetitive by design.
- Product promotion for LK's mentorship program / Prosperity School.
