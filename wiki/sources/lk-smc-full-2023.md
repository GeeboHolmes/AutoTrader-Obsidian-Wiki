## Summary
One of LK's earlier comprehensive SMC courses. Three-pillar framework: Market Structure → Supply and Demand → Multi-Dimensional Structure. The course introduces key terminology: "sell-to-buy" candle (OB), "mitigation" (OB retest), "realignment" (LTF CHoCH = signal that HTF pullback is beginning). The course builds to a 1:29.25 RR live trade example, demonstrating extreme profit potential from tight stop entry models.

Notable for introducing the "Range → Initiation → Mitigation → Continuation" framework (4 phases), which aligns closely with the later 5-phase model in lk-5-phase-manipulation. The key addition here: a single price leg may contain MULTIPLE Range-Initiation cycles, stacking multiple OB entry opportunities.

## Key Points
- **Pillar 1 — Market Structure:** HH/HL/LH/LL with the fractal nature across timeframes. BOS = close beyond swing (wick through = liquidation, NOT BOS). CHoCH = first BOS in opposite direction.
- **Pillar 2 — Supply and Demand:** OBs are "sell-to-buy" or "buy-to-sell" ranges — where smart money manipulated to fill orders. These ranges are protected (the institution doesn't want price above their sell or below their buy). The OB must have FVG (imbalance) to qualify.
- **Pillar 3 — Multi-Dimensional Structure:** 4H + 15m relationship. When 4H breaks structure and pulls back, the 15m will be bearish (downtrend on 15m = the 4H pullback). When the 15m shifts bullish (CHoCH = "realignment"), that signals the 4H pullback is complete and the 4H will now extend.
- **Trading Range Method:** Trading range = current swing high to swing low. Any price action inside the box is internal (pullback / noise). Wait for external breakout, then identify the new trading range.
- **Premium/discount:** Standard 50% Fibonacci. 80–90%+ retrace examples shown (price often goes to 50%, frequently to 80–90% retracement).
- **4-phase supply/demand cycle:** Range → Initiation → Mitigation → Continuation. Or: Range → Liquidation → Initiation → Mitigation → Continuation. Multiple cycles can stack in one structural leg.
- **1m confirmation:** 15m CHoCH → drop to 1m → look for same schematic: sell-to-buy with imbalance → enter from 1m OB.
- RR examples: 1:11 and 1:29.25 in the live trade. Variable RR depending on structure.
- **15x rule for timeframe selection:** Use timeframes that are 15–20x apart. 15m × 16 = 4H. Don't use daily and 1m together.

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. 4H market structure confirmed (BOS = candle CLOSE, not wick).
2. 4H breaks structure to downside (BOS). Now expecting pullback before continuation.
3. On 15m: structure should be bullish (realignment phase — 15m is the pullback in progress on 4H).
4. Wait for 15m CHoCH (lower low → higher high = realignment to downside when ready to continue 4H trend).
5. When 15m CHoCH occurs in a supply zone: confirm the OB inside this level.
6. OB must: (a) be sell-to-buy type, (b) have FVG (imbalance), (c) be inside the structural price leg.
7. Drop to 1m for same schematic: bearish on 1m, wait for 1m CHoCH inside the OB zone.
8. Enter from 1m OB. Stop below the 1m structural low. Target: next 4H structural low (trend continuation).

## Contradictions / Open Questions
- OB definition: "the sell-to-buy that has imbalance" — LK requires FVG to validate an OB. This is more restrictive than the later definition of OB (any last opposing candle before impulse). The FVG requirement likely filters out low-quality OBs.
- 1:29.25 RR from a 1m OB — this is possible but extreme. Normal trades in this system yield 1:5–1:12.
