## Summary
A motivational/educational video for new traders covering 7 key lessons LK wishes he had known earlier. Primarily psychology and business mindset content with no new technical trading rules. Key financial concepts: focus on assets under management (AUM) over percentage returns, realistic monthly return targets (5–10%), and the prop firm path to scaling capital.

## Key Points
- AUM > ROI: "The game of trading is actually a game of assets under management — it's how much money you have more than what percentage you can return."
- Realistic monthly returns: 5–10% per month, consistently. Not 50–100%.
- Path to $10,000/month: 5% on $200K or 2.5% on $400K — achievable through prop firms.
- S&P500 averages 8% per year; LK averaged 80%+ in 2024. Framing: retail traders comparing themselves to Lamborghini traders online are using wrong benchmarks.
- Prop firms: can access $500K+ in funded capital; keep 70–90% of profits.
- Seven things covered (framework): (1) proven map exists; (2) AUM > ROI; (3–7) mindset/process items.
