## Summary
A comprehensive step-by-step breakdown of LK's complete SMC strategy using a live trade. The strategy framework is: 4H timeframe provides the primary directional bias; Fibonacci retracement (50–78.6%) defines the pullback entry zone; FVG and supply/demand zones within that zone provide the specific OB; and 15m CHoCH confirms the pullback direction has shifted, enabling 1m entry timing.

The key mental model introduced here is "macro bias vs micro bias." The macro bias (4H: bearish long-term) may conflict with the micro bias (today: likely to pull back higher before continuing lower). Understanding both simultaneously is how LK avoids getting trapped fighting the micro while missing the macro opportunity.

## Key Points
- 4H CHoCH (break of swing low with candle CLOSE) = primary bias shift signal.
- After 4H BOS/CHoCH, use Fibonacci from the most recent swing high to swing low. Pull back into 50–78.6% = expected entry zone.
- Price "almost always" pulls back to at minimum 50%; most commonly 61.8–78.6% before continuing.
- FVG (3-candle imbalance) within the Fib zone is a major confluence for entry zone selection.
- Supply/demand zone (OB) overlapping with FVG at 61.8–78.6% = highest confluence area.
- 15m must confirm: if 4H is bearish and pulling back bullish, wait for 15m to CHoCH bullish (higher high) before entry. Only once 15m confirms the pullback direction do you look for entry.
- 1m entry model: drop to 1m when price enters the OB/FVG zone. Wait for 1m CHoCH. Enter from 1m OB.
- Minimum RR: 1:5. Target: next major HTF liquidity (BSL/SSL).
- Macro vs micro: "I have a larger term bias of lower prices, but today I believe price will pull back first."

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. Identify 4H directional bias — break of swing with CANDLE CLOSE (not wick).
2. On first CHoCH/BOS, draw Fib from swing high → swing low (for bearish pullback). Note: 50% = minimum retrace; 61.8–78.6% = premium zone for entry.
3. Identify FVGs within the Fib zone. Identify supply OBs overlapping with the FVG.
4. Wait for 15m to shift in direction of the pullback (15m CHoCH bullish = 4H bearish pulling back).
5. Drop to 1m when price enters the OB/FVG cluster. Wait for 1m CHoCH.
6. Enter from 1m OB. Stop below the 1m low. Target next major HTF liquidity level.
7. Confirm trade direction aligns with 4H macro intention (don't short a bullish pullback inside a bearish 4H).
