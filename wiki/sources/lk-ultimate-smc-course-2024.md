## Summary
Another complete SMC course walkthrough. This video is notable for its integration of FVG identification into the "area of interest" concept and for its explicit three-step structure (bias, area of interest, liquidity). It also covers the mechanics of why OBs work by explaining how smart money both artificially induces retail buying to get their sell orders filled, and how they can also simply stop accumulating orders to let retail push price into their zone. Live trade breakdown confirms the system works across EUR/USD.

## Key Points
- **FVG as primary area of interest:** Three-candle gap (candle 1 high < candle 3 low for bullish) = where institutional player stepped in
- **"Three candle sequence":** Range of candles → aggressive move → FVG between candle 1 wick and candle 3 low = the zone to watch
- **OB + FVG overlap:** The range before expansion (OB) contains the FVG — they are the same institutional zone described from two angles
- **Smart money induction mechanics:** Two ways institutions create liquidity for their orders: (1) artificially break a level to flood retail traders in wrong direction, (2) stop accumulating so price moves naturally into their zone
- **Step 3 is liquidity:** Identify retail liquidity (trendlines, equal highs/lows, support/resistance) → avoid buying before it's swept
- **Internal shift = entry trigger:** After price sweeps liquidity and returns to OB/FVG, wait for internal 1m CHoCH as confirmation

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Directional bias (Step 1):** 15m structure. Bullish = HH/HL. Bearish = LH/LL. CHoCH = first sign of potential shift.
2. **Area of interest (Step 2):** Identify range + aggressive expansion. Mark the three-candle FVG within that expansion. FVG = entry zone.
3. **Liquidity (Step 3):** Map all retail liquidity levels. Require that SSL has been swept before entering longs; BSL swept before shorts. Require target liquidity (BSL for longs) above price.
4. **Confirmation (Step 4):** Price enters FVG/OB zone. 1m structure shifts bullish (for longs). Enter from 5m FVG/OB at the level.
