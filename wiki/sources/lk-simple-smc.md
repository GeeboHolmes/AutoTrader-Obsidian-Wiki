## Summary
LK explains the core SMC intraday framework with emphasis on the *why* — institutional accumulation logic, why OBs form, why price returns to them. Features a $24,000 live trade example. Explicitly stated stats: 40% win rate, 1:6.4 average RR. The strategy is the 3-step base: 15m bias → POI identification → 1m CHoCH entry.

The pedagogical focus distinguishes this source: LK explains the full institutional cycle (accumulation → expansion → retest → continuation) and why OBs/FVGs represent unfilled institutional orders. The "seller exhaustion" model — consolidation after a push means the opposing side has absorbed all sellers, so a reversal is imminent — is central to LK's entry logic.

## Key Points
- $24,000 live trade example shown
- 40% win rate, 1:6.4 average RR
- Core 3-step structure: 15m bias → OB/FVG POI → 1m CHoCH
- Deep institutional logic: OBs = unfilled institutional orders; institutions must return to fill
- Accumulation → expansion → retest cycle is the core price delivery model
- Seller exhaustion = consolidation within OB → imminent reversal signal
- 1m CHoCH within 5m zone provides the sniper entry (same mechanism as 3-rule sniper)

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — 15m Directional Bias:** External structure only (box method). Bullish = HH/HL. Bearish = LH/LL. Determine which direction institutional flow is headed.
2. **Step 2 — Point of Interest (POI):** Identify OB (last candle before expansion move) or FVG (three-candle imbalance) in the direction of the bias. These represent institutional order zones where unfilled orders exist. Price is expected to retrace to these zones. Prefer areas where OB and FVG coincide.
3. **Step 3 — 1m CHoCH Within Zone:** When price enters the 5m OB/FVG zone, drop to 1m. Within the zone, the LTF will be trending counter to the 15m bias (e.g., 15m bullish → 1m bearish within the OB). Wait for the 1m to shift (CHoCH) back in the direction of 15m bias. Consolidation = seller/buyer exhaustion. CHoCH = confirmation. Enter at next 1m OB or candle open after shift; stop at 1m swing low/high; target 15m structural swing.
