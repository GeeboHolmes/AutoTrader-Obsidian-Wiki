## Summary
LK presents the five-stage trader roadmap: (1) The Call (entry), (2) Unconscious Incompetence (honeymoon phase), (3) Conscious Incompetence / Valley of Death (most quit here), (4) Conscious Competence (improving), (5) Unconscious Competence (profitable). The video focuses on how to move from stage 3 to stage 4 — the "Valley of Death" where 99% quit.

No new technical trading rules. Valuable as a pedagogical framework for understanding the learning journey.

## Key Points
- Five-stage roadmap: Call → Unconscious Incompetence → Conscious Incompetence (Valley of Death) → Conscious Competence → Unconscious Competence.
- Stage 2 (Unconscious Incompetence): everything feels easy, rapid apparent progress, unrealistic expectations take hold.
- Stage 3 (Valley of Death, Conscious Incompetence): you now know what you don't know; losses mount; most traders quit here.
- The valley: "99% of traders quit at level three."
- Stage 4 (Conscious Competence): you can trade profitably but it requires active effort and focus.
- Stage 5 (Unconscious Competence): profitability becomes automatic; the process is internalised.
- LK's own path: 7–8 years from start to level 5.
