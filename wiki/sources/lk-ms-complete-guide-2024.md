## Summary
Eight-step market structure masterclass (April 2024). The key new concept is the "true market reversal" rule: a CHoCH alone is NOT a confirmed reversal — it is a *potential* reversal. True confirmation requires CHoCH + pullback + BOS in the new direction. Until the BOS in the new direction occurs, the CHoCH is provisional and should be treated cautiously. Also introduces "multi-dimensional market structure" (step 7) — the concept that market structure is fractal and all timeframe structures must be read simultaneously.

## Key Points
- **True reversal 3-phase model:** (1) CHoCH — "potential reversal signal"; (2) pullback creating HL (if bullish CHoCH) or LH (if bearish); (3) BOS in the new direction — "confirmed reversal." Only at phase 3 should bias fully shift.
- **CHoCH alone = provisional:** A CHoCH can be a fakeout — price can CHoCH bullish and then CHoCH back bearish. Trading the CHoCH immediately leads to being stopped out on fakeouts. Waiting for the subsequent HL + BOS provides higher confirmation.
- **Conservative option:** For newer traders, skip trading the CHoCH and only enter after the confirmed reversal (CHoCH + HL/LH + BOS). Lower frequency but higher success rate.
- **Three phases of true market shift (Step 3):**
  1. CHoCH (lower low or higher high breaking previous structural point) — trend reversal signal.
  2. Price creates first HL/LH in the new direction — pullback within the new direction.
  3. Price breaks structure in the new direction (BOS) — full confirmation.
- **Equal highs/lows as liquidity** (reinforcement from 2023 content): equal highs create a cluster of sell stops; price will sweep them then continue.

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. Identify CHoCH on 15m or H4.
2. Do NOT enter immediately on CHoCH — it is provisional.
3. Wait for price to create HL (if bullish CHoCH) or LH (if bearish CHoCH) = first confirmation.
4. Wait for price to BOS through the initial CHoCH level = second confirmation.
5. Now bias is fully shifted. Enter at the next OB/FVG in the trade direction.
6. Alternative: enter on the HL/LH (after CHoCH, before BOS) with tighter stop — accepts lower confirmation but higher RR potential.

## Contradictions / Open Questions
- The "true reversal" rule adds a phase between CHoCH and confirmed bias — but most LK strategy pages use CHoCH directly as the bias trigger. The conservative approach (wait for BOS) reduces entries but increases accuracy. This is a strategy-level decision: trade CHoCH directly (faster, lower confirmation) vs. wait for full reversal (slower, higher confirmation).
