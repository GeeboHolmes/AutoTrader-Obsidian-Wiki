## Summary
LK presents his "A+ setup" — framed as the first strategy that made him profitable and the highest-confluence version of his intraday model. The strategy adds a daily candle bias layer on top of the standard 15m + Asia sweep framework, creating a three-layer confluence filter. LK's key claim: the daily candle character tells you what the next day will do.

The unique addition is the **daily candle reading system** — specific close/wick patterns on the previous day's candle predict next-day direction. A bonus step (LTF CHoCH) tightens the entry for a minimum 1:5 RR. LK calls this his best-performing setup due to the high-conviction triple-confirmation structure.

## Key Points
- LK's claimed "first strategy that ever made me profitable"
- Three-layer confluence: daily candle character + 15m bias + Asia sweep
- Minimum RR: 1:5 — same constraint as 4-step proven strategy
- Bonus step: 1m LTF CHoCH within OB/FVG for sniper entry → enables 1:5+ RR
- Daily candle character is the highest-timeframe filter; if daily says bearish, only take short setups
- Session windows: London (2-5am EST) and NY (7-10am EST) — same as other LK models

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Daily Candle Character (Bias):** Read the previous day's candle close relative to prior structure. Bearish signals: daily candle closes below previous day's low (bearish continuation), or daily wick extends above PDH then closes inside range (rejection). Bullish signals: daily candle closes above previous day's high (bullish continuation), or daily wick extends below PDL then closes inside range (rejection wick). Daily character sets the directional filter — only trade in that direction for the next session.
2. **Step 2 — 15m Intraday Bias:** Confirm the same direction on 15m external structure (box method — external swing highs and lows only). Bullish = HH/HL; bearish = LH/LL. Both daily and 15m must agree.
3. **Step 3 — Asia Sweep:** During London (2-5am EST) or NY (7-10am EST), wait for Asia session low (bullish) or high (bearish) to be swept. All three layers must align before any entry is considered.
4. **Bonus Step — LTF CHoCH + 1:5 RR:** After Asia sweep with triple confluence confirmed, drop to 1m. Wait for CHoCH in the trade direction. Enter at 5m OB/FVG from within the reversal zone. Minimum RR: 1:5 from entry to stop. If 1:5 not achievable, do not enter.
