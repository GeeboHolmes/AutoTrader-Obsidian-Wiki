## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Narrative building:** Higher timeframe is bearish (multiple bearish daily candles). Daily candle printing as bullish (green) = manipulation phase. This is the short entry opportunity — "sell the bullish candle."
2. **Asia session stacked liquidity:** Identify Asia low = current day low = equal lows with prior day = PDL. All three coinciding = massive stacked liquidity pool. This is the target.
3. **15m supply zone:** Identify the 15m OB (the range of candles where demand exhausted and sellers took over). This is the entry area when price retraces into it.
4. **1m confirmation:** When price enters 15m supply zone, go to 1m. Wait for sellers to take a 1m swing low (CHoCH bearish). This is the entry signal.
5. **DXY confirmation (optional):** During draw-down, check DXY. If DXY is bullish (FVG holding), EUR/USD will hold. Use this to maintain conviction and avoid panic closing.
