## Summary
Motivational/psychology video addressing why most traders quit before becoming profitable. No new technical SMC content. Key insight: most traders have unrealistic capital and return expectations — they expect to turn $1K into $10K/month when the math is impossible without insane leverage/risk.

## Key Concepts Extracted

### The $300K Math Reality
- Top 5% traders earn 3–5% per month consistently.
- To make $10K/month at 4.5% return: requires $222K+ capital.
- Most retail traders start with $1K–$5K and expect $10K/month → impossible without 200–1000% monthly returns.
- This expectation mismatch causes: over-leveraging → blowing accounts → quitting.

### Why Traders Quit
- Unrealistic return expectations relative to account size.
- They over-leverage to compensate for small account → blow up → conclude "trading doesn't work."
- The goal should be: develop the skill first, scale the account second.

### Top 5% Traits (LK's list)
- Accept that skill development takes time (1–2+ years).
- Focus on percentage returns and process, not dollar amounts.
- Scale capital after proving consistent edge, not before.
