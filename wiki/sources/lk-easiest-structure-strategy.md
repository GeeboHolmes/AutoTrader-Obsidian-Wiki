## Summary
LK presents a clean 4-step market structure strategy framed around a $26K live trade. Distinctive element: "sell a bullish candle, buy a bearish candle" — counter-intuitive daily context entry where LK deliberately enters short while the daily candle is still printing green (bullish), because the bearish structure context is so clear. Points of interest defined as "areas where demand exhausted and sellers took control" — the last interaction between buyers and sellers before momentum shifted.

## Key Points
- $26K in one session (multiple accounts); member Harry made $13K from same trade
- 4 steps: 15m bearish trend → Asia high taken → point of interest (supply OB) → 1m orderflow shift
- **"Sell a bullish candle":** enter short while daily candle is still printing as bullish/green — counter-intuitive but the bearish structural context makes it the ideal time (buyers creating the entry opportunity)
- **Point of interest definition:** the area where demand met supply and momentum shifted from bullish to bearish. The FINAL interaction between buyers and sellers before the sell-off. Sellers entered here; they have a vested interest to defend this level.
- 5 setups total in LK's system; this is setup #1 shown
- Asia session high = mandatory sweep trigger (same as base model)
- Entry: when price enters the 15m supply OB zone, go to 1m, wait for 1m swing low to be broken (sellers take control)

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — 15m Bearish Trend:** External structure. Swing high + swing low + break of structure to downside = confirmed bearish. Note: this swing high is the "strong high" and swing low is the "weak low."
2. **Step 2 — Asia Session High Taken:** Wait for London session to take out Asia session high. This is the manipulation phase. The sweep of the Asia high confirms that smart money is positioning for the real move (bearish).
3. **Step 3 — Point of Interest (Supply OB):** Identify the 15m supply zone = the area of candles where demand exhausted and sellers took control for the first time. The last bullish demand area before the bearish expansion. Mark as entry zone. Stop: above the OB high.
4. **Step 4 — 1m Orderflow Shift:** When price enters the 15m supply zone, go to 1m. Wait for 1m buyers to be in control first (normal retracement). Then wait for 1m sellers to take control — specifically: wait for 1m swing low to be broken (CHoCH bearish). This is the confirmation. Enter short at the 1m OB. Stop: above 15m OB high. Target: Asia low (or stacked liquidity target).
