## Summary
Primarily a product promotion video for LK's "Orthos Labs Market Structure OS" indicator. Limited original trading content. The core educational message articulates why traders lose as two root problems: wrong bias (direction) and correct bias with wrong timing. Both problems are claimed to be solvable by the same foundational understanding of market structure.

## Key Points
- Two root causes of all trading losses: (1) wrong directional bias, (2) correct bias but wrong timing.
- Both issues trace back to reading market structure incorrectly — specifically using internal structure as if it were external.
- The indicator promoted automatically labels real swing highs/lows, internal vs external structure, BOS and CHoCH across any timeframe.
- Limited new strategy content beyond the "bias + timing = market structure" framework.
