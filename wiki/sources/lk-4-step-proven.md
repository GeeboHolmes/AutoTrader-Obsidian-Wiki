## Summary
Lewis Kelly presents a 4-step intraday strategy with explicit live trade evidence: 577 trades, 360%+ return over 4–5 years (2021–2025), consistent green months in the 12–32% range. He frames the strategy as the result of one year of dedicated testing to arrive at the exact market structure reading methodology he now uses.

The strategy shares the same Asia-sweep mechanic as the 5-step version but reorganises into 4 steps with a unique fifth rule: minimum 1:5 RR required — the entry is adjusted within the OB zone (pulled lower for longs) until a 1:5 reward-to-risk is achievable. This mathematical constraint is the distinguishing feature of this specific model.

LK notes he has 7 variations of this strategy; this video covers 1 (London session, Asia low variant for bullish continuation).

## Key Points
- 577 trades, 360%+ return over 4–5 years (backtested + live)
- Anti-breakout philosophy: retracement entries yield 1:6–1:8 RR vs breakout's 1:1
- Focus on external swing points only — ignore internal structure for bias
- Asia session = 8pm–midnight EST (blue box); London = green box; NY = orange box
- Minimum 1:5 RR rule: adjust entry within OB until TP/SL ratio is at least 1:5
- "Don't care about old price action" — only the current swing high and low matter
- Fractal principle: same structure pattern occurs on every timeframe
- Stop loss: below the 1m swing low created at the CHoCH reversal point

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Directional Bias:** Use 15m market structure, external swing points only. Bullish = the most recent leg has broken the previous swing high. Identify current swing high (resistance) and swing low (support). Trade only in the direction of this structure; ignore all internal structure noise.
2. **Step 2 — Key Liquidity (Asia Low):** Wait for the Asia session low (8pm–midnight EST) to be taken below price for bullish setups (Asia high for bearish). Cannot enter long if Asia low has NOT been swept. This is the mandatory liquidity filter — statistically, entries before the sweep are losing trades in this model.
3. **Step 3 — Area of Interest (OB/FVG):** Identify an OB or FVG formed during the pullback into the entry zone. Definition: the final sell candle before an aggressive buy move (or vice versa). Use this as the entry area. Fractal logic applies — the OB on the 30m or 15m may also contain a smaller OB on the 5m or 1m within it.
4. **Step 4 — Entry Confirmation (1m CHoCH + 5m OB):** Wait for 1m structure to shift from bearish to bullish (1m CHoCH — higher high formed after a sequence of lower highs and lower lows). This is the "green light." Enter from the 5m OB at the level that gives minimum 1:5 RR to the swing high target. Pull entry lower within the OB if needed to achieve this ratio. Stop: below the 1m swing low at the CHoCH point. Target: external swing high (15m).
