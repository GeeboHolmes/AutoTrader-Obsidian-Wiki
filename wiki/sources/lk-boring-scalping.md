## Summary
LK presents the "Boring Scalping Strategy" — a session-range play built on the principle that London will sweep one side of the Asia range and then run to the other side. The thesis: Asia sets a consolidation range; London/NY manipulates one extreme before delivering to the opposite extreme. This is a daily cycle variation that LK describes as "consistent" and "boring" because it happens the same way every day.

The strategy has only 4 steps (though titled "3 steps" — the 4th appears explicitly in the transcript). The unique feature vs other LK strategies is the **target**: instead of targeting an external swing high, the target is always the **opposite Asia range extreme** (if Asia low swept → target Asia high). This makes it a short-term scalp with a defined, session-specific target.

## Key Points
- Core thesis: run one side of Asia → run the other side; daily cycle variation
- Session window: London 2-5am EST (primary); NY also valid
- Step 1: Wait for Asia sweep (either side taken by London open)
- Step 2: Drop to 1m, wait for market structure shift (CHoCH)
- Step 3: Enter at 5m FVG (or 50% retracement of FVG + OB confluence for shorts)
- Step 4: Target opposite side of Asia range
- Stop: below FVG low (longs) / above FVG high (shorts)
- Bearish example: Asia high swept → 1m shift bearish → 5m FVG/OB entry → target Asia low
- Result time: 10-15 minutes to hit target in examples shown

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Asia Sweep:** Identify Asia session range (8pm–midnight EST). Wait for London open (2am EST). If Asia low or high has been taken by London open, step 1 is complete.
2. **Step 2 — 1m Structure Shift:** Drop to 1m. Wait for CHoCH in the direction opposite to the sweep. Bullish sweep of Asia low → wait for 1m to shift bearish → then bullish CHoCH. Wait for price to take the most recent 1m swing high (longs) or low (shorts).
3. **Step 3 — 5m FVG Entry:** After 1m CHoCH, go to 5m. Look for FVG (three-candle imbalance) in the reversal zone. For shorts: can use 50% retracement (fib 50%) of the FVG as precise entry if OB and FVG coincide. Enter at FVG. Stop: below FVG low (longs) / above high (shorts).
4. **Step 4 — Target:** Asia session opposite extreme. If Asia low swept → TP = Asia high. If Asia high swept → TP = Asia low.
