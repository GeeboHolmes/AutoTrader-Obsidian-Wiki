## Summary
LK presents a concise three-step trading model: Impulse → Correction → Impulse (ICI). Step 1 is the M15 impulse (BOS). Step 2 is the correction (pullback with 1m structural shift to bearish/bullish as confirmation). Step 3 is the second impulse (the trade). Entry is taken when price reaches the M15 area of demand during the correction phase and the 1m order flow realigns bullish (for longs).

This is the most cleanly articulated version of the core LK strategy from 2023. The video derives from a live trade taken in London session on a Thursday. Minimum RR explicitly stated as 1:5.

## Key Points
- ICI model: Impulse (M15 BOS) → Correction (price pulls into OB/demand; 1m turns bearish) → Impulse (1m shifts bullish = entry).
- Step 1 requires recent M15 BOS (bullish for longs, bearish for shorts).
- Step 2 requires price to pull back into the M15 area of interest (OB/demand with FVG inside it).
- Step 3 entry: on 1m, observe price is bearish (correction visible on 1m), then wait for 1m CHoCH bullish (LTF order flow shift) → enter long.
- "Impulse contraction impulse — that is the exact trading model that I rinse and repeat time and time again." (end of video)
- M15 OB must have a fair value gap inside it (FVG = confirmation of imbalance).
- 4H context check: when 4H structure is also confirming the direction, the trade can be held for a deeper H4 target (not just M15 target).
- H4 as secondary take-profit: when H4 is aligned and bullish, target is H4 swing high (allows holding 20% of position for H4 move).
- Minimum RR: 1:5.

## Trading Rules Stated
1. Identify M15 impulse: a recent M15 BOS (bullish break) confirming directional bias.
2. Mark the M15 OB (demand for longs) — it must contain or be adjacent to a FVG.
3. Wait for correction: price pulls back into the OB while 1m is bearish (this is normal — the correction).
4. Entry trigger: 1m bullish CHoCH (order flow shift from bearish to bullish) inside the M15 OB zone.
5. Stop: below the M15 OB low (or below the 1m swing low at entry for tighter RR).
6. Primary target: M15 swing high (structural target). Take 80% here.
7. Secondary target: H4 structural high (if H4 is also bullish and aligned). Leave 20% of position for this.
8. Minimum RR: 1:5 before entering.
9. Move to break even when intermediate structural swing is taken out.
