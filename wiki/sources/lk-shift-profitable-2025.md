## Summary
Interview/documentary format video where LK discusses his personal journey to profitability. Primarily motivation and mindset content. Key insight: the shift that enabled LK to become profitable was abandoning social-media-inflated expectations (10R+ trades every day) and understanding that consistent 3–4% monthly returns are exceptional performance — and that the money comes from AUM, not from RR.

Also discusses the mental impact of FTMO unlimited days (previously 30-day restriction created psychological pressure that caused poor trading decisions), and the shift from "performance" to "process" mindset.

## Key Points
- LK hit 3% in month 1 and 4% in month 2 of FTMO and considered himself a failure because his benchmark was wrong (social media showed 20–30%/month as normal).
- 4% per month for a year = 48% annual return — a legitimate professional performance.
- "When you go well that same 3% on a million dollars is 30 grand, it becomes life-changing money — it's the same percentage, it's the same trades."
- AUM is the multiplier; RR/percentage is secondary.
- FTMO time pressure (old 30-day model) caused traders to force trades and take excessive risk. Unlimited days = dramatically easier psychology.
- Key shift: taking focus off social-media traders showing 1:100s and studying institutional/hedge fund average returns instead.
- LK has turned down broker affiliate deals (six-figure offers) to maintain integrity; highlight for teaching credibility.
