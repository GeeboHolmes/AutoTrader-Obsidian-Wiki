Unique addition: DXY (Dollar Index) as cross-reference confirmation when a setup is near a stop-loss level. 87 trades of data referenced. $100K+ in 2024 from this system.

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Directional Bias (15m):** External swing structure. Identify swing high and swing low. Bearish = LH/LL. Note: internal structure breaks (within the swing range) are NOT BOS — these trap retail traders.
2. **Step 2 — Manipulation Identification:** Before entering, identify the obvious liquidity level that institutions will run. Types: equal highs (two+ highs at same level), trendlines with 3+ touches, prior session high/low (previous NY high, previous London high), clear S/R levels. These will be swept before the real move. Wait for the sweep.
3. **Step 3 — LTF Multi-Timeframe Entry:** After manipulation sweep, drop to 1m. Identify the 15m supply zone (OB/FVG area). When price reaches the 15m supply zone, drop to 1m. Wait for 1m CHoCH (sellers take control of 1m within the 15m zone). Enter at 1m OB/zone. Stop: 3.9 pips above zone high. Target 1: structural lows (3.64 RR). Target 2: 1:5 RR.
4. **DXY Cross-Reference:** When EUR/USD is in deep draw-down (near stop loss level), check DXY (Dollar Index). If DXY is at a bullish FVG with momentum, EUR/USD is likely to hold and recover — this provides conviction to hold the trade. If DXY is bearish, reassess.
