## Summary
Video covering five principles for breaking into the top 1% of traders. Primarily mindset and business content. Key themes: avoiding fake gurus / signal services (broker b-booking mechanics explained), understanding the AUM game, realistic return targets (5–10% monthly), and the prop firm path to $500K+ in funded capital.

Notable technical context: broker b-booking explained — for CFD/forex brokers, when a retail trader goes long, the broker takes the short side (opposite order). The broker profits when the retail trader loses. This creates a conflict of interest with signal providers who are paid by brokers to funnel retail traders.

## Key Points
- 10 million new traders enter the market annually; 99% fail within 6 months.
- Signal services scam: broker pays signal provider to funnel retail clients; broker b-books (takes opposite side of every trade). Broker profits = retail trader losses.
- "For every long position that you open, they're taking the short position to fill your order."
- Not all educators are scammers: differentiate lifestyle marketers (show Lamborghinis/jets, no value) from actual educators (show process and knowledge).
- LK declined broker affiliate deals in the six-figure range to maintain integrity.
- AUM path: achieve $500K in funded capital → return 5% monthly = $25K/month. Keep 70–90% of profits from prop firm.
- Five principles to reach top 1%: (1) avoid fake gurus; (2) AUM > ROI; (3–5) mindset/process principles.
