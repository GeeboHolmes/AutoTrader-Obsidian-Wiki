## Key Concepts Extracted

### Responsible Low (consistent with 2026 version)
- CHoCH occurs when the *responsible low* — the specific swing low that produced the last structural high — is broken.
- Internal lows (within the swing range) are not valid CHoCH reference points.
- Consistent with the formulation in [[sources/lk-ultimate-smc-full-2026]].

### Three-Phase Reversal Model (consistent with 2026 version)
1. CHoCH (potential shift signal)
2. Higher Low forms (pullback from the new direction)
3. BOS (confirms the new direction is genuine)

Entry at phase 2 (HL) or phase 3 (BOS) depending on risk tolerance.

### Wicks = Liquidation (consistent)
- Wicks through a level that close back inside = liquidation event, not structural break.
- Only candle *closes* confirm BOS or CHoCH.

### Internal vs External Structure (consistent)
- Do not change bias based on internal structure signals.
- Only external range breaks (BOS/CHoCH of the dominant swing) justify bias change.
