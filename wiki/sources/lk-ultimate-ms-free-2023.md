## Summary
LK's most comprehensive November 2023 course — described as possibly the most valuable video on his channel at the time. Covers all three types of structure (swing, order flow, internal pro-trend), introduces the "multi-dimensional" (multi-timeframe) model, and walks through two live trade breakdowns (Ben's NY open trade and LK's London trade).

Key naming conventions introduced here: "internal counter" (IC) = order flow during pullback, "internal pro" or "internal pro-trend" (IP) = order flow aligned with swing (OFR signal). The three-phase reversal model is given more precise language: CHoCH → HL/LH → BOS (three confirming events before confirming a new trend).

The video also introduces the concept of the "equal lows liquidity trap" — when a POI has equal lows below it, the OB may not hold because price will continue through to clear the equal lows. LK's response: disregard the OB in favour of the level below, or accept that price may tap and run equal lows before the reaction.

Model 2 (multi-dimensional, M15+M1) is the primary practical model: M15 identifies swing + OB zone → M1 order flow shift provides entry confirmation. Risk: below OB low. Target: M15 structural swing target.

## Key Points
- Three-phase reversal: (1) CHoCH — first sign of shift; (2) mitigation/HL or LH formation; (3) BOS — confirms new trend.
- Three structure types: swing (external reversals), internal counter (IC — pullback phase), internal pro (IP / OFR — realignment signal).
- "Internal counter" = price making HH/HL internally while swing is bearish → this is the pullback.
- "Internal pro-trend" (IP) = internal structure realigns with swing → this is the entry signal.
- Equal lows below an OB = "equal lows are completely indicative of a run on liquidity" — expect price to sweep equal lows before reacting to OB.
- When equal lows exist below the POI, do not place entry at the OB — instead, consider the next deeper POI.
- M15 swing low target: the primary target for all bearish trades is the M15 structural swing low.
- Minimum RR: 1:5. LK will not take a trade unless at least 1:5 is achievable.
- Partial exits at 80% at first major target (previous structural swing); leave 20% to run to H4 target.
- Move to break even when price takes out the intermediate structural swing low.

## Trading Rules Stated
1. Three-phase trend confirmation: CHoCH → HL (or LH) → BOS. All three must occur to confirm new trend.
2. Don't change bias on CHoCH alone — it is necessary but not sufficient. Wait for the HL or LH and then the BOS.
3. When the M15 is bearish and M1 is bullish → M15 pullback phase. Wait for M1 internal shift to bearish (IP signal) before entering.
4. Equal lows below POI = liquidity magnet. Expect price to sweep them. Either wait for the sweep or use a deeper POI.
5. Minimum RR = 1:5 before entering any trade.
6. Take 80% of position off at M15 swing target; leave 20% for H4 or deeper target.
7. Move stop to break even when M15 intermediate structural level is broken.
8. Trade session window (London only; occasionally into New York if trade is already open and break-even secured).
