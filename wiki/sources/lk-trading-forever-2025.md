## Summary
Personal narrative video. LK describes his own journey from analysis paralysis and FOMO-driven trading to systematic, edge-based trading. No new technical SMC content. The "1 thing" is having a documented, backtested, rule-based trading plan — executing consistently rather than reacting emotionally.

## Key Concepts Extracted

### The Analysis Paralysis Problem
- LK describes spending hours analyzing charts, second-guessing setups, missing entries due to hesitation.
- Root cause: no clear rule-based plan → subjective judgment required on every trade → mental fatigue.

### The Shift: Systematic Edge
- Developing a written, rule-based strategy with defined entry/exit criteria eliminates hesitation.
- When the rules are met, you execute. When they aren't, you don't.
- No discretion required → no emotional friction.

### FOMO as a Symptom
- FOMO (fear of missing out) is caused by lack of a plan — trader doesn't know which setups are valid.
- With a clear plan: missed setups are irrelevant because the next one will come.
- With no plan: every move feels like a missed opportunity.
