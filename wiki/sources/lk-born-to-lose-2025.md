## Summary
Psychology-focused video arguing that the human brain is biologically wired to fail at trading — optimised for survival (avoid pain, seek safety) rather than probabilistic decision-making under uncertainty. LK introduces the "survival paradigm" concept as the root cause of trading failure: the amygdala hijacks rational decisions mid-trade, causing fear/greed responses that override the plan.

No new technical content. Valuable for trading bot design context: the system removes the amygdala entirely — this video implicitly supports automated trading as a solution.

## Key Points
- "Survival paradigm" = the outdated mental operating system that causes fear/greed responses in trading.
- Amygdala (emotional brain) hijacks the rational plan when in a trade. "Your plan wasn't in charge. Your amygdala was."
- Fear manifestations: closing trades early, hesitating at entry, moving stop losses.
- Greed/FOMO manifestations: chasing entries after missing signal, adding to losing positions.
- Breaking free from survival paradigm requires intentional rewiring — not more strategy, not more discipline.
- Two invisible forces destroying trading: fear and greed, driven by the survival paradigm.
- Not a discipline issue — it is a brain architecture issue.
