## Summary
Lewis Kelly presents a 5-step intraday SMC strategy built around the Asia session as a predictable liquidity pool. The core thesis is that institutional participants systematically take out Asia session lows (for longs) or highs (for shorts) before reversing into the killzone direction. This sweep-and-reverse pattern is the filter that separates valid setups from noise.

The strategy is explicitly session-structured: Asia (8pm–midnight EST) sets the liquidity range; London (2am EST) and New York are the trading windows. The strategy combines HTF directional bias (15m external structure) with a mandatory Asia sweep filter and LTF confirmation (1m CHoCH → 5m OB entry).

## Key Points
- 5 explicit named steps with strict ordering
- Asia session = 8:00pm–midnight EST (blue box)
- London = 2:00am EST; New York session follows
- You CANNOT enter a long before Asia low is swept — this is the mandatory liquidity filter
- Wick ≠ structure break; candle close required for CHoCH/BOS
- "Strong high" = most recent high that caused a break of the previous low
- Entry at OB high; stop at OB low (5m timeframe)
- Target = session highs/lows, PDH/PDL, trendline liquidity

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Daily Bias via 15m Structure:** Identify the external swing range on the 15m. Bullish = HH/HL sequence; bearish = LH/LL. Only trade in the direction of 15m external structure.
2. **Step 2 — Asia Session Sweep:** Wait for the Asia session low (bullish bias) or high (bearish bias) to be taken out. Asia session = 8:00pm–midnight EST. Do not enter until the sweep occurs. Wick sweeps are liquidations — only candle closes count for structure.
3. **Step 3 — Structure Shift:** After the Asia sweep, wait for a 1m CHoCH in the trade direction (opposite to the sweep). Bullish: 1m forms a higher high after the sweep.
4. **Step 4 — Order Block:** Identify the 5m OB — the final bearish range (for longs) immediately before the aggressive bullish expansion. Entry = OB high; stop = OB low. This is the range before the momentum candle.
5. **Step 5 — Liquidity Target:** Target the nearest significant liquidity: session highs/lows, PDH/PDL, or trendline liquidity. Target must be clean — pre-existing resting orders.
