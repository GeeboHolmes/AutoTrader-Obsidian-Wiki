## Summary
LK diagnoses why traders fail even with good strategies — the "failure cycle": find strategy → take trades → win → overconfident → lose streak → abandon → repeat. The root cause is not the strategy but three missing elements: proper preparation (backtesting and paper trading), psychology/process (following the plan), and patience (waiting for the right setups).

No new technical content. Reinforces that strategy alone is insufficient without the surrounding process infrastructure.

## Key Points
- Failure cycle: Find strategy → excited → take live trades → initial wins → overconfidence → losing streak → abandon strategy → find new strategy → repeat.
- Three missing elements: (1) backtest/paper trade before live; (2) build psychological infrastructure; (3) patience for A+ setups.
- "98% of traders get stuck in this one failure cycle."
- Strategy is not the problem. Process is the problem.
- Transition: demo/paper trading → small live account → scale.
- "If Elon Musk handed you the plan to build a rocket do you think that you could do it? Probably not because it's not just about having the strategy — it's about knowing how to execute every single step of the process."
