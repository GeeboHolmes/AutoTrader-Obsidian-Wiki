## Summary
LK presents the strategy that made his first $100K — a 3-step backtested system using premium/discount 50% Fibonacci as the primary entry filter with a fixed 1:3 RR for all trades. Two entry models: (1) discount reversal — long at structural lows when price is in discount, (2) premium continuation short — short at OB when price retraces into premium. Same 1:3 fixed RR for both. Backtested live on EUR/USD 15m.

## Key Points
- "First strategy that made me $100K"
- 3 steps + backtested
- Primary filter: premium/discount (50% Fibonacci of current structural price leg)
- **Discount = buy zone:** only look for longs when price is BELOW 50% of the current price leg
- **Premium = sell zone:** only look for shorts when price is ABOVE 50% of the current price leg
- **Two models:**
  1. Pullback reversal (discount): after BOS to downside, price in discount → wait for 1m shift + OB retest → long to 1:3
  2. Premium continuation short: price in premium of bearish leg → 1m shift + OB retest → short to 1:3 or swing target
- Fixed 1:3 RR on all trades — no discretion
- Partial at 1:3 then hold remainder for swing (optional)
- London 2-5am EST, NY 7-10am EST; can extend to 11am if setup brewing
- 1% risk per trade

## Trading Rules / Setups Described
1. **Step 1 — Direction (15m):** External market structure. Identify swing high and swing low. BOS confirmed by CLOSE (not wick). Bearish = price closes below swing low. Set directional bias.
2. **Step 2 — Premium/Discount Filter:** Draw 50% Fibonacci of the current structural price leg (from swing high to swing low). Above 50% = premium (sell zone). Below 50% = discount (buy zone). Only trade in the appropriate zone for the direction.
3. **Step 3 — LTF Shift + OB Entry:**
   - **Discount reversal (long):** Price in discount. Drop to 1m. Wait for 1m price to make a lower low, then shift bullish (1m CHoCH — swing high broken). The OB/range cluster that caused the shift = entry zone. Enter at the open of that range. Stop: 4-4.5 pips below range low. Target: 1:3 RR fixed.
   - **Premium continuation short:** Price in premium. Drop to 1m. Wait for 1m to push lower (CHoCH bearish). Enter at the 1m OB cluster. Stop: 4-4.5 pips above. Target: 1:3 RR fixed; optional hold remainder for swing structural low.
