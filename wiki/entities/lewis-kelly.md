## Overview
Lewis Kelly is a SMC trading educator with a large YouTube channel focused on Smart Money Concepts, market structure, order blocks, fair value gaps, and complete trading strategies. He teaches a practical, rule-based approach to SMC trading applicable to forex and crypto markets.

## Teaching Style
- YouTube-based; high-volume content output.
- Strategy-oriented: videos frequently present complete step-by-step strategies with specific rules.
- Practical: uses real trade examples and backtested results to validate strategies.
- Accessible language — translates SMC concepts into actionable rules for retail traders.
