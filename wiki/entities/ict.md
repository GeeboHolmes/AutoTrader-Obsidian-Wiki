## Overview
ICT (Inner Circle Trader) is the trading educator identity of Michael J. Huddleston. He is the originator of the core Smart Money Concepts framework and the ICT methodology, which has become the dominant institutional price action framework in retail trading communities.

## Key Contributions
- Original formulation of [[concepts/order-blocks|Order Blocks]]
- [[concepts/fair-value-gap|Fair Value Gaps]] (FVGs / Imbalances)
- [[concepts/liquidity|Liquidity]] theory (BSL/SSL, EQH/EQL)
- [[concepts/market-structure|Market Structure]] / [[concepts/break-of-structure|BOS / CHoCH]] framework
- [[concepts/premium-discount|Premium / Discount]] model
- Optimal Trade Entry (OTE) — Fibonacci-based entry at 61.8–79% retracement
- Power of 3 (PO3): Accumulation → Manipulation → Distribution (AMD)
- Killzones: London Open (02:00–05:00 NY time), New York Open (07:00–10:00 NY time)
- IPDA (Interbank Price Delivery Algorithm) — ICT's theory of how price is algorithmically delivered
- Silver Bullet setups (specific time-based entries)
- Unicorn Model
- New Day Opening Gap / New Week Opening Gap

## Teaching Style
- YouTube-heavy; large free content library.
- Terminology is proprietary and specific — when ingesting ICT content, use ICT's exact terms.
- Some concepts have definitions that differ slightly from "generic SMC" — always note the source.

## Relevance to This Project
ICT's framework is the primary methodology being implemented in this trading bot. All core concept definitions default to ICT's definitions unless a source explicitly departs from them and a better alternative is documented.
